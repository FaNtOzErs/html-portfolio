Spaghetti with Minced Meat

Classic Italian pasta with meat sauce.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Minced meat
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Boil spaghetti
  2. Cook minced meat with onion
  3. Add tomato sauce
  4. Mix with spaghetti

Lasagna

Layered pasta with meat and cheese.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Minced meat
  • Cheese
  • Tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Cook meat
  2. Layer sheets, meat and cheese
  3. Bake for 30 minutes
